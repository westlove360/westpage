EXAMINATIONS

One of the core mandates of the Council as espoused in the enabling Act is the conduct of the Senior School Certificate Examination for internal and external candidates. Other examinations conducted by the Council are: National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE) and Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE)

The Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE) Internal, is the examination conducted for candidates at the end of their Secondary School Education and the SSCE External is conducted for candidates that have attempted and have deficiency(ies) to make-up.

National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE)

The National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE) is the examination meant for admission into the Junior Secondary School 1 of Federal Unity Colleges.




Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE)

The Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) is the examination conducted for candidates of Federal Unity Colleges and other allied institutions who have completed their Junior Secondary School education.
